Exelon Generation

Solar Farm Technician 1

Jan 2014 - July 2015

In this position I was an upper level technican at a 240MW solar farm in Rosamond, CA. I would troubleshoot substation issues, central inverter issues and tracker issues at the site. I was also responsible for tracking degregration of the panels at the farm and perform dirty/clean analysis to know when to have the site washed for better performance. I would also perform QA/QC work on 3rd party contractors that were called in for big issues. I would monitor plant performance and when inverters were not performing as expected, I would troubleshoot and bring them back to expeced production.

Iberdrola Renewables

Wind Farm Technician

Jan 2012- Jan 2014

In this position I worked at the Manzana Wind Farm. This site consisted of 126 GE 1.5MW wind turbines that were tied into SoCal Edison. I was one member of a 12 man crew. I was assigned an inexperienced technician and I was responsible for training him. I performed annual maintenance including torque checks, blade re-calibration, generator alignments and firmware updates. I was part of a rotating on-call schedule and would dispatch to the site at all hours of the day.
