Work Related Certifications/ Achievements

SMA Inverter Certification

In 2015, I received a certificatin from SMA Solar to work on their inverters. This is a level two certification that shows that I am a qualified technician to do in-depth troubleshooting in both firmware/software and physical issues. SMA is the number 1 solar inverter brand in the world with annual sales in excess of 25 million units. Nationally Recognized
